About Us

My culinary journey began during the lockdown, inspired by Korean dramas. This led to the creation of Aamchi, combining my name with kimchi to reflect my passion for this versatile dish. I share recipes online to inspire others and offer small, medium, and large jars of kimchi for delivery across the UK, crafted with fresh ingredients for authentic flavors.
